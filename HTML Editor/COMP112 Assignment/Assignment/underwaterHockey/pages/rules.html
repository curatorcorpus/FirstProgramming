Rules

  1. Games are typically 30 minutes long with a short break between halves.
  2. At the beginning of a game, the two teams start on the surface touching the pool side at their respective ends.
  3. The puck is placed in the middle of the playing area.
  4. At the sound of the gong, whistle, bell or hooter the game will start.
  5. The puck is propelled or passed from player to player using the stick only
  6. No off-side rules applied
  7. The puck can be passed in any direction
  8. No contact is allowed between the two teams.
